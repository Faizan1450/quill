Privacy Policy

Quill – Smart LinkedIn Replies is committed to user privacy. Our philosophy is simple: your data is yours alone.

  • 1

    Zero Server Operations

    Quill does not operate any databases, analytical servers, or third-party storage clusters. The developer cannot see, access, collect, or store any of your inputs, preferences, or generated comments.

  • 2

    Local-Only Preference Storage

    Your API key, configured tone, length, writing style descriptions, and custom comment examples are saved strictly on your local device inside your browser's extension storage (local storage). They never leave your machine except during generation.

  • 3

    Direct Gemini API Transmission

    When you click generate, the post content is packaged alongside your API key and sent directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API endpoints. The request goes directly to Google; there are no proxy servers or intermediaries involved.

  • 4

    No Analytics or Tracking

    We do not use Google Analytics, cookies, telemetry tracking, or advertisements. Quill does not monitor how or when you use the extension.

  • 5

    Contact & Support
